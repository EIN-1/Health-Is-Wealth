About Us


Our Story

Founded in 2020, Health is Wealth is a community fitness group of people who meet up for fitness and networking. We care about our body, soul, and mind when working out and aim to improve our health at the same time. We work out together at different intervals throughout the week, allowing everyone the flexibility to join us when free or off-duty and work out at a time that suits them.
